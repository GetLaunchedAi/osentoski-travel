About

Travel That Brings Learning to Life

I’m Samantha — a mom, wife, and dog lover with a lifelong travel bug. Coming from a military family and after years in the classroom, I decided to turn my passion into a full-time career helping others explore the world.

Through Osentoski Travel, I combine my background in education with expert planning to create meaningful, learning-rich experiences for school groups and unforgettable adventures for families.

"The best memories are built through shared experiences. My goal is to make travel easy, educational, and inspiring — one trip at a time."

Samantha Osentoski Owner, Osentoski Travel
