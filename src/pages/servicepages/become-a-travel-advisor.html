Want to Become a Travel Advisor FAQs

Answers to common questions about becoming a travel advisor through our mentorship program by Osentoski Travel.

  • No prior travel industry experience is required. Our mentorship program is designed for beginners and covers everything from basic travel booking to advanced business strategies. A passion for travel and helping others is the most important qualification.

  • Earnings vary based on effort, client base, and specialization, but successful travel advisors can earn significant income through commissions, fees, and residual income from repeat clients. Our mentorship focuses on building sustainable, long-term income streams.

  • We provide comprehensive training on booking systems, vendor relationships, client management, and business development. You'll also receive ongoing support, access to exclusive resources, and guidance on building your client base and growing your business.

  • Most mentees can start booking travel within 2-4 weeks of beginning the program. Building a sustainable business typically takes 6-12 months of consistent effort, but the timeline varies based on individual goals and dedication.

  • Many successful travel advisors start part-time while maintaining other commitments. Our mentorship program is flexible and can be adapted to your schedule and goals, whether you want to build a full-time business or earn supplemental income.
